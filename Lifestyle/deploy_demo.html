📱 Mobile-Only Deployment Demo

Option 1: GitHub Pages (Recommended)

  1. Open https://github.com
  2. Sign up free (2 minutes)
  3. New repository: name lifestyle-tracker
  4. Upload all Lifestyle files
  5. Settings → Pages → Deploy from branch
  6. Get URL: https://yourname.github.io/lifestyle-tracker

Option 2: Netlify Drop (Instant)

  1. Open https://app.netlify.com/drop
  2. Drag all Lifestyle files onto page
  3. Get instant URL (no account needed)

iPhone Setup

  1. Open your URL in Safari
  2. Share → Add to Home Screen
  3. Works offline after first load

🔥 Mobile-Only Benefits

  • Works anywhere, anytime
  • No laptop needed after deployment
  • Data stays on your iPhone
  • Works offline (PWA)
  • Automatic backups to Google Drive

Total setup time: 5-7 minutes
Then use forever from your iPhone only!
